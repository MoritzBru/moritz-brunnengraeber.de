Who I am

I am a 26 years old and live in Puchheim, a suburb of Munich.
At the moment, I am a master student in Geodesy and Geoinformation at the Technical University of Munich.
In my free-time I like keeping fit, playing video games and hanging around with friends, especially with my girlfriend.

What I do

Webdesign

I like building modern and responsive websites with Wordpress or Bootstrap while improving my CSS and JS skills.

Photography

I love photography: underwater, on the ground or in the air, night or day, long time exposure, time-lapse or just a normal shot.

Drones

I enjoy flying with my drone making aerial photographies and especially fully spherical aerial panoramic images.

Maps

Recently, I discovered how much fun it can be to design and build interactive web-based maps with frameworks like Mapbox.
